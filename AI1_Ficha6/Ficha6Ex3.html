Sarah Parmenter

Sarah Parmenter is a well-known web designer and developer based in the UK. She has made significant contributions to the field of web design, specializing in user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design. Sarah is the founder of You Know Who, a design studio, and she has worked with various international clients. She is also a frequent speaker at web design conferences, sharing her expertise with the design community. Her work has inspired many designers to focus on creating intuitive and aesthetically pleasing digital experiences.
